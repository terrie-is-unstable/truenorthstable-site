Policy

Boarding

Our care emphasizes consistency and communication. Stalls are cleaned daily and turnout is planned with temperament, footing, and weather in mind. Owner-provided supplements are fed on request.

  • Daily stall cleaning and fresh bedding as needed
  • Quality hay & grain; clean, fresh water
  • Individual or small-group turnout
  • Blanketing by schedule/season

Facilities & Amenities

Comfortable, practical spaces for horses and riders. Safe footing, tidy aisles, and organized storage help everyone focus on good horsemanship.

  • Indoor and/or outdoor arenas with maintained footing
  • Round pen and designated lunging areas (where applicable)
  • Tack rooms and lockers
  • Trailer parking by arrangement

Trainers & Clinics

True North Stable does not provide in-house training services. Boarders typically ride with their own trainer. Visiting trainers and clinicians are welcome with prior approval.
